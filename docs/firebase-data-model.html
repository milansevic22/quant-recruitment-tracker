Firestore Collections

Three simple collections cover the MVP: tracked companies, discovered jobs, and scan-run history.

companies

FieldDescription
idStable company identifier.
nameCompany display name.
careersUrlPublic careers page to scan.
keywordsRole keywords used for matching.
activeWhether the scanner should include it.
createdAtCreation timestamp.
updatedAtLast updated timestamp.

jobs

FieldDescription
idDeterministic job identifier.
companyIdReference to the tracked company.
companyNameDenormalized company name.
titleDiscovered role title.
locationBest-known location or remote note.
urlExternal job posting URL.
sourceUrlCareers page where the role was found.
firstSeenAtFirst detection timestamp.
lastSeenAtMost recent detection timestamp.
statusnew, seen, applied, or ignored.
updatedAtLast admin review update timestamp, when changed through the protected status route.
keywordsMatchedKeyword matches from the title or link text.
roleTypeGraduate, internship, quant, trading, research, engineering, or other.

FieldDescription

newJobsAddedNew unique jobs inserted.
