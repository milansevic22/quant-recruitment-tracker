Product Brief

Quant Recruitment Tracker

A focused internal tool for tracking relevant early-career roles across quant trading firms, hedge funds, and market makers.

Problem

Quant recruitment roles appear on many company careers pages, often with different naming conventions and posting systems. Manual checks are repetitive, easy to miss, and hard to summarize for follow-up.

Target User

A candidate, operator, or internal talent scout who wants one place to review relevant quant, trading, research, and software roles.

Personal Use Case

Track graduate and internship openings across a focused list of firms and quickly decide which roles are new, relevant, applied, ignored, or worth checking manually.

Reusable Product Use Case

The same structure can support other recruitment watchlists by changing the tracked companies and keyword set. The MVP keeps the scanner generic enough for future career-track configurations.

MVP Scope

  • Dashboard with summary cards, tracked companies, and jobs.
  • Firestore-backed company, job, and scan-run collections.
  • Protected seed route for realistic starting data.
  • Protected scan route for safe public-page checks.
  • Mock fallback data when Firebase is not configured.

Out of Scope

  • Logged-in multi-user workflows.
  • Automated application submission.
  • Bypassing bot protection, CAPTCHAs, logins, or paywalls.
  • Complex ranking models or AI scoring.
  • Production alerting guarantees.

User Workflow

  1. Open the dashboard and review summary counts.
  2. Scan discovered roles by firm, role type, status, and keyword.
  3. Open external job links to verify fit.
  4. Seed or scan through protected admin routes as needed.
  5. Use scan-run records to understand recency and failures.
